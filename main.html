Welcome to Bono Region Youth Parliament

The collective voice of young people in the Bono Region

We are dedicated to promoting youth development, leadership, entrepreneurship, and community building across all districts of the Bono Region. Through advocacy, skills training, and community projects, we empower the next generation of leaders.

Our Vision

A united and prosperous Bono Region led by empowered, skilled, and visionary youth who drive sustainable development.

Our Mission

To mobilize, equip, and empower young people with leadership skills, entrepreneurial opportunities, and platforms to actively participate in regional development.

Core Values

Unity, Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Service to Community
